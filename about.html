About Me


I was born on 7th October 2005 in Skopje. I finished elementary school at OOU "Dimitar Pop Geogriev Berovski".

I completed secondary school at SUGS "Georgi Dimitrov", in the special German-language class. During that time I took part in several regional competitions in math, physics and geography.
In October 2023, I participated in the Western Balkans Youth Conference "Berliner Prozess goes Schule". During the event, I attended lectures on "The Media Landscape in the Balkans" and "Social Media", participated in a workshop on fake news, and worked with a group of students to create a short video on the topic.

In October 2024, I officially started my first year of undergraduate studies at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering — FINKI at UKIM, enrolled in the Computer Science (KN) program. So far, it's been an exciting and challenging journey.
